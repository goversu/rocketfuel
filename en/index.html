rocket fuel

we are a non-exclusive agency managing music for content creators and streamers. whether you're just starting out or are an industry veteran, we can distribute your music worldwide and pay you what you deserve.

why choose rocket fuel:

  1. non exclusive
    sometimes, creators need resources that we cannot provide effectively or are outside our area of expertise. we understand that. you are free to work with or sign with any other entities, and withdraw from rocket fuel with no penalty anytime
  2. we take 0%
    we’re tired of seeing other agencies take large percentages from their talents, where the only value they provide is a small boost in views and an upfront advance. the only payment you have to make at rocket fuel is the international distribution fee of $18 a year (cheapest on the public market). if you make enough money from royalties, we will internalize this cost and you will only ever have to make 1 payment in your lifetime when signing with us
  3. project funding
    upfront costs for music projects can be very daunting. we fund projects that we think will be profitable and take royalties from the release until we make the money back (it’s a 0% interest loan + your risk is absorbed and you get expert guidance!!)
  4. earn interest
    transfer methods incur large fees, often a % + fixed fee, and a large fixed fee for wire transfers. many creators choose to hold money with their distributors until they need it or it adds up to sizeable amounts. at rocket fuel, we don’t just let your money rot there. we give you 2% per annum interest on any of your money we hold, and you can submit a withdrawal request anytime at no additional fee (there is a distributor fee of $1 + paypal fees)
  5. networking
    we have access to a wide network of talents, producers, writers, artists, singers, etc., and can find just the collab or service you need to create something amazing. don’t underestimate the potential in combining the abilities of like-minded, passionate creatives! in addition, you can get advice from experienced industry professionals; so many opportunities for growth
