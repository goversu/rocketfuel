business model

seems too good to be true?

not really, the other companies just need more money because they have to hire lots of staff. here's how we make money:

  1. distribution fee
    we pay less than $18 per year per person to distribute your music, so we make a small profit there. we think it is fair to charge $1.50 a month especially since it's the lowest rate on the public market for individuals
  2. investments
    we invest your balance in liquidity funds regulated by monetary authority of singapore, with an average of 2.27% per annum returns since inception (as of september 2024), with extremely low risk (risk is taken by us, you always get 2% p.a.)

in addition, as we are a registered business, we also get tax benefits whenever we fund your projects and pay your distribution/license fees
